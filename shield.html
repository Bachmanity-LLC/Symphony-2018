Antakshari

  • Description

    For all music enthusiasts among teachers and students, come and participate in this amalgamation of different forms of diverse music styles and genres in a captivating musical antakshari sing-off. So join along for Symphony Shield Antakshari and make everyone sway and hum along as you go through the finger-biting experience of being in the hot seat.

  • Rules

    1. Event Category: One team per branch
    2. Compulsory Participation: 2 students + 2 faculty per department (including SAHAS)
    3. Rolling of participants is allowed.

Art

  • Description

    “Art isn't supposed to look nice; it is supposed to make you feel something.”

    ― Rainbow Rowell


    Art can be visually satisfying, art can be thought provoking, art can be stirring, art can be moving. The Symphony Shield Art Competition invites works of fine arts in all forms. Hold your branch name the highest in the field of arts.

  • Rules

    1. Event category: Multiple team per branch
    2. Compulsory participation: 1 drawing per branch.
    3. Theme: Kaleidoscope of Ages. Theme is open to interpretation.
    4. Minimum Size of the drawing: A4
    5. The drawings should be submitted by 1st February, 2018.and to be collected within 2 weeks after Symphony.
    6. 1 person can submit maximum upto 2 drawings

Dance

  • Description

    “Dance is the hidden language of the soul”

    ― Martha Graham

    Dance to express, dance to impress. Showcase your department’s ingenuity and pit yourself against the titans of dance. It's time to steamroll the competition with your breathtaking and jaw-dropping inter-departmental dance performances.

  • Rules

    1. Event Category: One team per branch
    2. Participation compulsory from each year : 2 FYs, 2 SYs, 2 TYs are compulsory.
    3. Number of Members per team: 7-15 members
    4. Time limit → 5-12 mins
    5. A face off which will consist of Solo performance of all branches together will happen at the end of all group performances.
    6. Individual dance → 1 minute + All 5 branch individual performance together → 1 min.

Debate

  • Description

    “Don't raise your voice, improve your argument."

    ― Desmond Tutu

    Prove your department's oratory genius in the battle of the most revered form of the Speaking Arts. Ethos, Pathos, Logos. Dig deep for that perfect riposte and bring all your arsenal to the battlefield to test your debating acumen and emerge as the ultimate champions of language and wit.

  • Rules

    1. Event category: Multiple team per branch
    2. Number of members per team: 6
    3. Compulsory participation: One FE, SE, TE and BE.
    4. Others can also participate, provided both the partners are from same branch. Therefore there can be any number of participants from one branch
    5. Elimination rounds will be a week prior to Symphony.
    6. Semis and Finals will be held during Symphony

Drama

  • Description

    “Act well your part; there all the honour lies.”

    ― Alexander Pope, An Essay on Man

    Earn the honour on the stage and prove your department’s weight over others. Here is a pedestal to showcase that your department can express each strain of emotion.

  • Rules

    1. Event category: One team per branch
    2. Number of members per team: 7-12 members
    3. Compulsory participation: 2 from FY, SY and TY each
    4. Time limit: Maximum 16 minutes which includes 1 minute for setup and clearance (inclusive).
    5. Theme: Kaleidoscope of Ages. Theme is open to interpretation.
    6. Three mics will be provided. Props will not be provided.
    7. Only one non-participating assistance will be allowed at the music controlling console.
    8. Use of props, script and music should be notified by 1st February 2018.
    9. Illustrations are not allowed.

Music

  • Description

    “Ah, music. A magic beyond all we do here!”

    ― J.K. Rowling

    Does your department have it in them to take centre stage and letting the musical magic unfurl? With all eyes on you, can your department strike all the right chords to make the crowd sway to your Symphony in this ultimate interdepartmental music battle!

  • Rules

    1. Event category: One team per branch
    2. Number of members per team: 3-7. Rotation is allowed
    3. Compulsory participation: A team can comprise of members from any year
    4. Time limit: 20 minutes including sound check, instruments tuning etc.
    5. Only basic mic, speakers and a drum kit will be provided to the participants. Amplifiers, instruments, etc, to be brought on your own.
    6. nstrumental for 1 min is necessary

Photography

  • Description

    “The artist sees what others only catch a glimpse of.”

    ― Leonardo Da Vinci

    Photography is that art which is not easily perceived through normal eyes. An observation and a story that you want to portray through your eyes to the world. Symphony Shield Photography invites you all to be a part of this artsy journey and put forth your vision for the Odyssey of Dreams.

  • Rules

    1. Event category: Multiple team per branch
    2. Compulsory participation: 1 photo per branch per year.
    3. The participants must get their own digital cameras. (Phone Cameras are allowed)
    4. Theme will be disclosed on the day of the event.The photographs should be submitted on the end of the day itself.
    5. No time stamps
    6. Basic editing is allowed. (Only Contrast, Brightness and Saturation can be adjusted during post effects
    7. Each photo should contain a caption of max 200 characters
    8. 1 person can submit maximum upto 2 photographs.

Quiz

  • Description

    Is your branch the most knowledgeable? Is your branch the wittiest of all? Let out your inner Watson (not so Elementary, that one) and prove it in the Symphony Shield Interdepartmental Quiz. Battle it out in this war of wits and take your department on the path of glory.

  • Rules

    1. Event category: Multiple teams per branch
    2. Number of members per team: 2
    3. Compulsory participation: One FE, SE, TE and BE.
    4. Others can also participate, provided both the partners are from same branch. Therefore there can be any number of participants from one branch.
    5. Elimination will occur a week prior to Symphony.
    6. Semis and Finals will happen on the day of Symphony.

Jamboree

  • Description

    Sometimes all you need is to break the routine and have a bit of fun. You have in store 3 days of fun activities, evenings of magic and splendour and a lot more. Are you ready to come on board the Cruise of Fun and Excitement because at the end of the day, it is an interdepartmental battle.

  • Rules

    1. Event Category: One team per Branch
    2. Compulsory Participation: 2 FYs, 2 SYs and 1 TY each.
    3. Team should contain : 6 members per team.
    4. Rotation of participants is not allowed
    5. The details of the event will be disclosed on the day.This event will start from Day 1 of Symphony and continue up to the Shield Closing.
