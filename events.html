Somaiya's Got Talent

Celebrating the best of talents in Somaiya, Somaiya's Got Talent, is back. With your passion, conviction and enthusiasm, showcase your talent at the Somaiya's Got Talent platform and stand a chance to win the Ultimate Talent Show in Symphony 2017.

  • Round: Eliminations + Finals
  • Team Size: 1-15

General Rules

  • Participants can perform anything.
  • Participant will be penalised on exceeding the time limit.
  • Obscenity of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Any kind of fluid, flame or heavy object(which may be dangerous) is not allowed on stage.
  • Participants have to mail the following to kjscesymphony@somaiya.edu:
    • The type of talent
    • Production List (List of production/prop material and music.Anything else brought by the participants ).
    • The subject of the mail should be "Somaiya's Got Talent". The deadline for submission is 29th January, 2017 11:59 PM

Elimination Rules

  • Time Limit: 3mins
  • Participants can perform a part of their final sequence for elims

Final Rules

  • Time Limit: 7 mins including stage setup and mic check if required.
  • No instruments will be provided by us.
  • A maximum of 5 mics will be provided.

Judging Criteria

  • Stage Presence
  • Innovation
  • Energy
  • Overall Impact
